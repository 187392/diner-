Weekly Chef Special

Dry-Aged Ribeye Steak

Experience the peak of tenderness and rich flavor. Our 16oz prime bone-in ribeye is dry-aged for 28 days, seared at 800 degrees to lock in natural juices, and topped with locally forged wild mushroom compound butter. Served with a side of truffle roasted potatoes.

Chef Marcus Vance

Head Chef & Co-Founder

For over two decades, Chef Marcus has believed that great meals do not need to be overly complicated. After studying in Paris and cooking in award-winning kitchens across coastal Italy, he returned home to start The Simple Diner.

"True culinary art lies in simplifying, not complicating. If you source fresh local ingredients and treat them with respect, the flavors speak for themselves."

Marcus Vance
